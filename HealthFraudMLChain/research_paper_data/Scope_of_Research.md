# VI. SCOPE OF RESEARCH

The scope of this research focuses on the design and development of an AI-powered health insurance fraud detection platform named HealthFraudMLChain. The system aims to reduce fraudulent claims and financial losses by integrating machine learning-based fraud detection, blockchain-based immutable record-keeping, multi-layer security architecture, role-based access control, and real-time notification modules. It also includes collusion detection algorithms, insider threat monitoring, digital signature verification using ECDSA, and sensitive data protection using ECIES asymmetric encryption. The research emphasizes building a scalable, secure, and regulatory-compliant platform that provides automated fraud scoring, explainable AI predictions, and comprehensive audit trails, helping insurance companies, healthcare providers, and regulatory bodies enhance fraud prevention, claim verification accuracy, and overall operational efficiency.
